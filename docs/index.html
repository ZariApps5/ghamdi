Privacy Policy

App: Quran – Al-Ghamdi  |  Package: com.zariapps.quran.ghamdi  |  Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Quran – Al-Ghamdi application ("the App") handles information when you use it on your Android device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). There is no registration, login, or account system. All audio content is bundled directly in the App and plays entirely offline.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised advertising via your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

3. Internet Permission

The App requests the INTERNET permission solely for the purpose of loading advertisements through AdMob. No audio is streamed; all Quran recitations are stored locally within the App.

4. Data Storage

The App stores minimal data locally on your device only:

  • Your favourite surahs (stored in a local database, never transmitted)
  • Your last playback position (stored locally, never transmitted)
  • Dark mode preference (stored locally, never transmitted)

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. AdMob is configured to serve ads appropriate for general audiences.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: zariapps@gmail.com
